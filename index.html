Sanghyuk Moon

About

I am a postdoctoral research associate at Princeton University. Performing numerical simulations and interpreting the results, I am trying to understand how prestellar cores spontaneously form out of turbulent molecular clouds. I did my Ph.D. at Seoul National University, where I studied gas dynamics and star formation in centers of barred galaxies.

Contact: sanghyuk.moon at princeton.edu

Athena++ with Open BC Self Gravity

A version of the Athena++ code with self-gravity can be found in https://github.com/sanghyukmoon/athena-public-version. It contains the Poisson solver based on the James algorithm that deals with the open boundary condition. Both 3D Cartesian and 3D cylindrical coordinates are supported. The method paper can be found in Moon, Kim, & Ostriker (2019).
